About

SHREE AGARWAL SAMAJ (IP colony Sector 32 Faridabad) was established in 1995 under the patronage of Sri Ravi Prakash Khemka who made major initial contribution for Samaj properties for a social and noble cause. At present Shree Agarwal Samaj is situated in a prime location of city of Chennai and its premises comprises of a hall, five rooms, Kitchen, Store room, office room, and two units of 3BHK for guest accomodation. The rooms are all furnished and are being used for Agarwal communities for their social event and temporary stay. These facilities are available for the community at reasonably minimum charges. Those who travel from other places and come here for treatment or Darshans, and also those who need space for cultural, social, marriage, religious etc events, make use of these facilities and it has been our privilege to serve them in all possible manner and provide comfort to their satisfaction.

Our moto is to create an atmosphere where Agarwals can communicate with one another without any hesitation and achieve the target in the field of business, social, medical, religion, sports, education etc. We are a part of the society in large and we welcome the suggessions for development and achievement of our objective.
